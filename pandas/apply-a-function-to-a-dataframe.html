Apply a Function to a DataFrame

  1. 16 October 2018
  2. Pandas

Import libraries

import numpy as np
import pandas as pd

Create data

df = pd.DataFrame({'First': np.random.randint(1, 6, 5), 'Second': np.random.randint(4, 10, 5)})
df
First Second
0 2 8
1 2 8
2 5 8
3 1 9
4 2 4

Apply a function across each row

df['Added'] = df.apply(lambda row: row['First'] + row['Second'], axis=1)
df
First Second Added
0 2 8 10
1 2 8 10
2 5 8 13
3 1 9 10
4 2 4 6

Apply based on one column

df['Squared'] = df['Added'].apply(np.square)
df
First Second Added Squared
0 2 8 10 100
1 2 8 10 100
2 5 8 13 169
3 1 9 10 100
4 2 4 6 36
